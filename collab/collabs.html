The "In Progress" tag does not mean a map is being collaborated on. In this case it means I am currently lighting this song already! The "Finished" tag can mean two things, if its accompanied with an open tag the lights are finished, if it has a closed tag instead the map was already taken and published! If a map has no description just know its going to be lighting.
